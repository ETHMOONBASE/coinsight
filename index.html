reddit user flairs

On reddit, users can set their "flair", a styled piece of text next to their name. For the /r/BitcoinMarkets community in particular, users set their flairs to Bullish or Bearish, depending on what their outlook is for Bitcoin. The goal of this sub-project is to determine whether the combined wisdom of these flairs can serve as a useful predictor for the price of a bitcoin. To encourage usage of the flair system, users are ranked below based on their performance in "the flair game."

the flair game

Rules and other information for the flair game can be found on this /r/BitcoinMarkets wiki page.
